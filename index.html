Recipe for Ham Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 1 strip of ham
  • Butter or Margarine
  • 2 slices of your preferred bread (black or white)
  • 1 knife

Steps to follow

  1. Grab your bread and spread it out on a plate.
  2. Take your knife and dip it in the butter or margarine
  3. Spread your butter or margarine onto the two slices of bread evenly
  4. Lay the pieces of ham neatly on top of the buttered bread.
  5. Take both pieces of hammed bread and mash them together
  6. You now have a ham sandwich
